Grab Wallet Chrome Extension

Vote with your dollar

This extension uses the registry from https://grabyourwallet.org/ to flag business websites that are associated with the Trump family's commercial activities.

The tool is targeted towards those that do not want to further enrich the Trump family for a variety of reasons, including unprecedented conflicts of interest in America's top office and the fact that self-enrichment is arguably one of Trump's top priorities and therefore, one of the stronger levers available for collective resistance.

Not shopping at these websites is only one of many possible ways to discourage companies from doing business with Trump. Other recommendations are available here: https://grabyourwallet.org/Your%20Recommended%20Actions.html
